## Hub Management Structure
Use this structure to keep content clean as the knowledge hub grows:

<div class="hub-grid">
  <article class="hub-card">
    <h4>1) Add new items by topic</h4>
    <p>Publish updates under the matching topic page instead of creating ungrouped standalone notes.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="hub-card">
    <h4>2) Keep each section modular</h4>
    <p>Use consistent modules like snapshot, implications, and action points for easy scanning.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="hub-card">
    <h4>3) Archive and rotate</h4>
    <p>Move dated content into archive blocks while keeping current decision-useful entries at the top.</p>
  </article>
</div>
