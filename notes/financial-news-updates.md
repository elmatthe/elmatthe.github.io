## Update Template
Use this template for each new update item:

<div class="callout">
  <p><strong>Date:</strong> YYYY-MM-DD</p>
  <p><strong>Headline:</strong> One-line summary of the most important development.</p>
  <p><strong>What changed:</strong> Key facts and data points.</p>
  <p><strong>Why it matters:</strong> Portfolio, planning, or risk-management relevance.</p>
  <p><strong>Action:</strong> Follow-up steps, if any.</p>
</div>
