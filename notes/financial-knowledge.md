## Practical Knowledge Blocks
<section class="hub-section">
  <h3>Asset Allocation Decision Stack</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Define objective, horizon, and constraints.</li>
    <li>Set strategic mix and acceptable drift bands.</li>
    <li>Select implementation vehicles by cost, liquidity, and tax fit.</li>
    <li>Review outcomes against risk and plan progress.</li>
  </ol>
</section>

<section class="hub-section">
  <h3>Risk Communication Principles</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Translate volatility into expected range language for clients.</li>
    <li>Separate short-term market noise from long-term plan risk.</li>
    <li>Document assumptions and explain uncertainty explicitly.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
